Infants Got Byte

This level is available for infants and children to support the lifelong process of exploring and learning. We allow each child to follow their natural temp to determine daily activities. The Infant Got Byte level maintain a ratio of 8:3

Music and Evolution: Providing musical instruments, and toys that sing and dance which helps stimulate babies and children to learn creates enjoyment.

Exploring: allow children to explore outside to better physical and mental health.

Motor Play: Helping children perform daily function activities.

Children Matters: Infant Got Bytes community is never too young to learn about keeping hands clean. Teaching children about this responsibility at a young age, will allow them to imply good hygiene in their daily routines.
